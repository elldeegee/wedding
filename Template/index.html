The "Real World"

May 2013
Finally together in NYC

After extended periods of time apart, from semesters abroad in Rome and Hong Kong, and Lauren's senior year at Cornell (with Marc in NYC), we finally entered the "real world" together after Lauren's graduation. We each started (and left) our first jobs, and got to know each other's roommates (a little too well), as we rarely spent nights apart. A special thanks to Melissa (and Ranger) for letting Marc become an "unofficial" tenant of 10 Hanover Square!

Los Cabos, Mexico January 2018

The Proposal

January 9, 2018
A vacation like no other

It started off like a regular vacation in Cabo San Lucas - days filled with fitness activities and lounging by the pool. Little did Lauren know that this vacation would be anything but regular! On the way out to dinner one evening, Marc suggested attending a tequila tasting hosted by the hotel. After a short walk, the only thing in sight was a beautiful sunset over the Sea of Cortez and a lantern surrounded by rose pedals. Marc got down on one knee, and Lauren said yes! The rest of the night was spent celebrating over a Mexican feast (including tequila, of course)!

Tidbits

What is your favorite thing to do together?

A perfect day for us is a long bike ride around the streets of Quogue, followed by dinner at one of our favorite Long Island restaurants, and finished off by a stop at Snowflake Ice Cream Shoppe in Riverhead, NY.

What was the first movie you watched together?

Mildly embarrassed to admit that the first movie we watched together was the 2004 dramedy Garden State, although we’re still not sure if Lauren stayed awake past the opening credits!

Who does more household chores?

Marc by a mile!

What's your most memorable date?

This one is tough, but it has to be our full day celebration for our 5th anniversary. The day started with waterskiing in Peconic Bay, followed by brunch and apple picking on the North Fork. It was so memorable that we've made waterskiing an anniversary tradition.

Who is the better golfer?

This may be the most controversial question of all. Although Marc (thinks) he’s better with his driver, Lauren definitely has the prettier overall swing!
